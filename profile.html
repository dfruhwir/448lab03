Trevor Phillips


Before his rise to fame in the legendary videogame Grand Theft Auto V, Trevor Phillips was an "ordinary" man. He was born in good ole Canada. As a child, he had a slight temper leading to fairly frequent run-ins with the law. Sick of being around kind citizens, he moved to the great city of Los Santos. There he grew his criminal empire from trailer home, eventually "earning" well over $40 million. Walter White couldn't compare to his greatness. Trevor likes to hang out with his partners Michael, Franklin, Cletus
